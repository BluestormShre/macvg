Boxel Rebound

About

Boxel Rebound is a poopular Chrom extension platformer game, brought to the web by MacVG. In this fast-paced reaction game, you need to control a block to jump over obstacles, spikes, and cliffs. Try to reach the end as fast as possible! As you advance, levels will be getting harder and there will also be other techniques like trampolines, speed up blocks, and resizers. Jump at the right time to avoid dying and get as many points as possible! Other than the preloaded 50 levels, you can also create your own unique fun levels and enjoy it yourself or with friends! Have fun!
