Arcane Archer
OnRush Studio

About

Arcane Archer is a fun RPG game where you control a powerful archer to defeat enemies on each stage and advance through different levels! The archer will aim and shoot automatically, all you have to do is to dodge attacks from enemies. You earn xp for each defeated enemy and you can choose new abilities and upgrades using the xp! There are many different and unique levels to play, so good luck guiding your archer through this adventure!
